Server Features

We are rebuilding the backend so we can all log back in and play our favorite modes.

Saved Rooms

Player-built rooms and inventions are completely preserved. The Maker Pen still works, meaning your creations are safe.

Multiplayer

Play with old friends and make new ones. The social tools and voice chat you remember are being brought straight back online.

Original Modes

Paintball, Quests, and Jumbotron. They are the classics for a reason, and you can play them on our server natively.

VR & PC

Full support for Meta Quest and PCVR, plus flatscreen mode for PC players. No expensive headset needed to join the party.

Open Source

You don't have to trust a massive corporation anymore. The server code is being built openly by us, the players.

Moderated

We elect our own moderators to keep the RecUnited lobbies clean and safe from trolls, just like the good old days.
